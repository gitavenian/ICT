WEB Content Filtering Solution

Zero-Latency Filtering, Safety and Compliance

Our WEB Filtering Solutions offer real-time layer 7 analysis, managing and regulating traffic within 100/40/10G Ethernet/POS network environments. To enhance networking reliability, an optical tap can be utilized to indirectly capture network packets for traffic analysis, irrespective of the network equipment type.

The Importance of URL Filtering

As the number of illegal and harmful websites continues to grow, coupled with the increasing volume of network traffic on both fixed and mobile networks, there is a heightened necessity for URL filtering. In response to these challenges, governments in the United States and Europe have implemented laws and regulations specifically targeting child pornography.
